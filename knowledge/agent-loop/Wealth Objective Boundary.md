# Wealth Objective Boundary

The desk can improve process quality, evidence velocity, risk-adjusted decision quality, and long-term compounding discipline. It cannot guarantee vast wealth or make financial outcomes certain.

The operating objective is:

> Preserve survival and optionality while seeking positive, verified, risk-adjusted expectancy.

The objective is not:

> Maximize short-term cash at any risk because lifestyle plans or emotional wellbeing feel urgent.

## Fixed rules

- Emotional urgency never increases position size or authority.
- A wealth target never substitutes for evidence.
- No candidate, paper result, open profit, balance change, or social-media claim is treated as guaranteed income.
- Mental wellbeing is not collateral for a trading outcome.
- When evidence is weak, preserving cash is a valid successful decision.
- Live orders remain explicit human decisions after all gates pass.

Steamboat, Aspen, and time freedom are legitimate goals. The reliable path is to protect downside, improve earning and saving capacity, compound patiently, and treat speculative upside as optional—not required for psychological safety.
